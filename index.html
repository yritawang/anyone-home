Have I been ghosting you?

My bad. I do that a lot. I don't mean to. Drop me a message right here: in my room. And my roommates' rooms as well. Leave us a message.

Click the doors to enter rooms, view message boards, and leave messages. Click arrows to navigate the hallway. Messages you write stay on the board until cleared.

About

Camera obscura brings the outside into the interior. With this project I hope to open my mind and my space to my surroundings more.

This project is created with html, css, and js. The photos are taken by me on Sandy Woon's Canon digital camera.
